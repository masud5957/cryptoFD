## Design System Specifications

### Colors
- **Primary**: #6C63FF (Purple)
- **Green Success**: #22C55E
- **Blue**: #3B82F6
- **Amber**: #F59E0B
- **Text**: #1A1A2E (light) / #E5E7EB (dark)

### Typography
- **Font**: Geist (sans), Geist Mono
- **Headings**: Bold, 2xl-4xl sizes
- **Body**: Regular, muted-foreground for secondary

### Spacing
- **Border Radius**: 2xl (premium), xl (components), lg (secondary)
- **Padding**: p-4 to p-8
- **Gaps**: gap-3, gap-4, gap-6

### Interactions
- Hover border transitions to primary/50
- Shadow enhancement on hover
- Smooth transitions (transition-all)
- Copy feedback ("Copied!" confirmation)
